Risk Model & Methodology
▼
Composite Risk = Pathway Volume × Pest Origin Risk
Pathway Volume: BTS T-100 air passengers + freight (2022–2025 avg, monthly)
Pest Origin Risk: EPPO Global Database — 3 tiers:
  3 = widespread  2 = restricted  1 = few/transient
Cargo Scaling: freight (lbs) ÷ 5,000 before combining with pax
Validation: APHIS PPQ interception records + IPPC phytosanitary reports
Host data: USDA FAS GATS fresh fruit & vegetable imports
